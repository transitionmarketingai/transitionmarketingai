- Revenue: $350/month

### Break-Even Analysis

**Fixed Costs** (Monthly):
- Development team: $10,000
- Tools & services: $2,000
- Office & admin: $1,000
- **Total**: $13,000

**Variable Costs** (Per Customer):
- Lead generation: $60
- Platform costs: $10
- Support: $7
- **Total**: $77

**Break-Even Customers**:
```
Fixed costs / (Revenue per customer - Variable cost per customer)
= $13,000 / ($350 - $77)
= 48 customers

At 48 customers, you're profitable!
```
